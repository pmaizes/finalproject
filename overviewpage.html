Overview


Just like the black plague, measles outbreak and Swine ‘09 there is a new pandemic that the entire world has become aware of: COVID-19. The coronavirus came and stayed in many parts of the country and across the globe. While the doctors work towards a vaccine, Dr. Anthony Fauci and other health care workers, have promoted and encouraged the public to wear masks. Dr. Fauci has said, “When you have crowds of people together and you have the lack of wearing a mask that increases the risk of there being transmissibility. I have no doubt about that.” (CNBC, "Halftime Report")





Social interaction is a large part of humanity. It has been proven throughout the years, so it is unsurprising that the public has become restless at home. In addition to the stay at home order placed in some states and countries, many stores and franchises are also closed for the time being. This ongoing isolation has led to an increase of depression symptoms in adolescents. To combat this depression, adolescents have discovered ways to socialize safely and increase their happiness with different activities.


"An idle brain is the devil's workshop."

- H.G. Bohn, Hand-Book of Proverbs, 1855


This quote means that while an individual is in quarantine, their mind is able to wander and slip away from reality. It does not help that jobs and businesses were closed for a period of time, beacuse individuals are meant to keep their minds active. But once they become idle and not busy, the brain starts ruminating and doubting everything, from themselves to factors that have been apart of that indivual's life. As a result, thinking too much which can make anyone go insane.
